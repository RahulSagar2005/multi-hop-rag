How it works

Three steps from document to verified answer

01

Document → Knowledge Graph

Paste any document. The LLM extracts entities (people, orgs, concepts) and their relationships, then stores everything in Neo4j with full source provenance.

Entity: GraphRAG TECHNOLOGY
Entity: Darren Edge PERSON
└─ Rel: WORKS_FOR → Microsoft
02

Query → Multi-hop Traversal

Your question seeds a graph walk — expanding outward through connected entities across N hops, assembling a rich, relationship-aware context.

Query: "Who developed GraphRAG?"
→ Hop 1: Darren Edge, Ha Trinh
→ Hop 2: Microsoft Research
03

Answer → Verified Citations

Every claim in the generated answer includes an inline citation [N] mapped to the exact source text and document it came from. Nothing is unverifiable.
